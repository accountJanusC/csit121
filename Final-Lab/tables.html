Final Reflection cont'd

Revisiting the Table and Form Lab that I couldn't submit on time, I saw that it required a ranking system which I found to be rather hard to complete since I generally don't like ranking things. So I just offered two tiers in this table, ranking each dissident by whether they have significant coverage in the international stage or are more lesser-known. I learned from W3Schools that the 'border-collapse: collapse;' property prevents tables from having more than one border. One thing I had a bit of trouble with was making sure each column and row remained consistent, and the labelling of 'Header 1', 'Row 2', 'Cell 1' etc. in the Table and Forms Notes in Module 07 helped me with this.
